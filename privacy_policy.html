Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and how we may use it on esycrm.com (and app.esycrm.com) Site. If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please feel free contact us at support@esycrm.com

Personal data

When you sign up for an account in esycrm we ask for your e-mail address and a password which will become your login credentials to sign in to esycrm.

We will do a best effort to deduce a user’s display name from a user e-mail address. Users are able to change their display name any time at esycrm Settings page.

Cookies

esycrm uses a technology called “cookies” to store session information. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site’s or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. They are also used to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services. We also use cookies to help us compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future. We also use Facebook Pixel for targeted ads

Payments

Payments are handled by external service providers (“Chargebee” and “PayPal”). You will be redirected to service provider website over secure link and you will enter all information necessary for payments there in secure manner. After payment you will be redirected to esycrm automatically.

esycrm will not store your Credit Card number. esycrm will store your name, billing address and/or VAT code for receipt display purposes.

Newsletters and other e-mail deliveries

As part of the service esycrm is sending out occasional newsletters. Your name and e-mail address are used for sending the newsletter. You can opt out of receiving a newsletter by clicking a link within the newsletter itself.

esycrm will send a welcoming email. We also send out invitation e-mails to people you have invited to join your team. You can always request a password recovery email from esycrm.

We might email account administrators for billing related purposes.

We will occasionally send out announcements related to esycrm service in general.

Blog

If you use the esycrm blog, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there (by commenting) can be read, collected, or used by other users of these blogs. esycrm is not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these blogs.

Data Security

esycrm operates over Secure Sockets Layer protocol (HTTPS), so that the information exchange between you and esycrm is encrypted. esycrm only uses a valid SSL certificate from major service providers.

We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and we will use for these new purposes only data collected from the time of the policy change forward. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.
